About Me

After graduating from Butler University, I went to work in the financial services industry. While I enjoy what I do, I love learning new things and stepping outside my comfort zone. Recently, I made the decision to enroll in Eleven Fifty Academy's Web Development program and I am gaining skills and knowledge every day. I'm learning that there are nearly limitless possibilities when it comes to what can be accomplished through web development. I aim to create websites that are simple, beautiful, and functional, and my hope is that my projects will make someone's life a little easier or day a little better. In that way I seek to make a positive impact through my work.
